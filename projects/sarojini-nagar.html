Brief & Approach Site Analysis Design Strategy Impact (SEE)

1. Brief and Approach

Sarojini Nagar is a market and government housing area, located within the South West district of Delhi, well-known for its markets for clothing, amongst others. This is a large neighborhood area of 246.167 acres comprising mostly low-rise residential blocks, with ageing structural integrity.

With the continual influx of new residents into Delhi from across India, there is a need of increasing suitable housing provisions. The Union Govt of India approved a budget of Rs 33,000 crores for a strategic framework in 2016 for improving seven different neighborhoods.
